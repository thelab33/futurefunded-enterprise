# FutureFunded objection handling

## "We already have a donation page."
That makes sense. The difference here is not just collecting payments — it is presenting the organization more credibly and making sponsor support easier to sell.

## "We are small."
That is actually where polish matters most. Smaller groups often lose support because the experience feels improvised. A stronger surface can increase trust quickly.

## "We do not have time to set this up."
The platform is built around guided launch and fast setup. The goal is to reduce setup burden, not create more admin work.

## "Our sponsors usually just donate directly."
That still works. FutureFunded simply makes sponsor visibility more structured so there is a stronger reason to commit and a cleaner package to present.

## "Can this work for our school / nonprofit / club?"
Yes. The core model is flexible: branding, fundraising story, sponsor lanes, and launch flow can all be adapted to different organization types.

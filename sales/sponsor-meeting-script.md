# FutureFunded sponsor meeting script

## Goal
Help a local business understand that sponsoring is not just a donation — it is a visibility and community-support opportunity.

## Script
Hi {{first_name}},

We are helping programs launch cleaner fundraising pages through FutureFunded, and one of the big upgrades is that sponsor support is packaged more intentionally.

Instead of a business just being thanked once, sponsors can be positioned with clearer visibility across the live fundraising experience. That gives local businesses a more concrete reason to participate while still supporting something meaningful in the community.

We currently frame sponsor support in simple package lanes:
- Community Sponsor
- Featured Partner
- Founding Partner

The goal is to make it easier for a business to understand what support looks like and why it matters.

Would you be open to a quick look at a live example?

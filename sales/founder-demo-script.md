# FutureFunded founder demo script

## Goal
Show that FutureFunded is not just a fundraiser page. It is a founder-ready system for:
- launching fast
- collecting donations
- selling sponsor packages
- creating a polished branded experience
- operating with less chaos

## Demo flow

### 1. Open with the public fundraiser
Link: `/c/spring-fundraiser`

Say:
> This is the live fundraising surface. It is branded, mobile-first, sponsor-friendly, and built to help a program raise money without feeling homemade.

Point out:
- clean top chrome
- trust and sponsor credibility
- visible fundraising momentum
- clear CTA path
- premium mobile feel

### 2. Show sponsor positioning
Say:
> A lot of groups ask for support, but most do not package sponsor value clearly. FutureFunded gives them a better sponsor story, not just a donate button.

Point out:
- sponsor wall / sponsor visibility
- featured partner language
- sponsor package lane
- invoice-friendly positioning

### 3. Open onboarding
Link: `/platform/onboarding`

Say:
> This is where a founder or operator can launch a program quickly — logo, colors, campaign setup, payments, and go-live flow.

Point out:
- guided launch framing
- speed to first publish
- low setup friction

### 4. Open pricing
Link: `/platform/pricing`

Say:
> This is where the business model becomes real. We are not just giving people a page; we are giving them a system they can launch and run.

Point out:
- founder setup CTA
- sponsor package value
- upgrade / white-glove potential
- premium positioning

### 5. Open demo or dashboard
Link: `/platform/demo` or `/platform/dashboard`

Say:
> This is the operator story. The product is meant to help a real organization run cleaner, not just look nicer.

Point out:
- founder readiness
- operator confidence
- repeatable rollout potential

## Close
Say:
> The core promise is simple: launch fast, look premium, collect support confidently, and give sponsors a reason to say yes.
